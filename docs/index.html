What Zonnie does

🌞

Real solar position

A precise algorithm places the sun in Amsterdam's sky for any date and hour. No approximations.

🏗

Shadow casting

LIDAR-derived building heights are projected to determine which terraces are blocked — and which are glowing.

🌤

Live cloud cover

Open-Meteo provides Amsterdam weather in real time so scores reflect actual sky conditions.

📍

900+ terraces

Bars, restaurants, and specialty coffee shops — curated by hand across every Amsterdam neighbourhood.

☕

Morning to evening

Coffee filter for sunny mornings. Bar/restaurant filter for afternoons. The right venues at the right hour.

🔒

Zero tracking

No account, no analytics, no ad SDKs. Your data stays on your device. Always.

How it works

01

Pick a time

Use the time scrubber to set any hour today, or let it default to now.

02

Scores update

Each terrace gets a 0–100 sun score based on solar angle, building shadows, and cloud cover.

03

Pick your spot

Filter by neighbourhood or venue type, tap a terrace for its hourly timeline, head out.
