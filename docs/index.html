What Zonnie does

🌞

Real solar position

A precise algorithm places the sun in Amsterdam's sky for any date and hour. No approximations.

🏗

Shadow casting

Building heights are projected to determine which terraces are blocked — and which are glowing.

🌤

Live cloud cover

Open-Meteo provides Amsterdam weather in real time so scores reflect actual sky conditions.

📍

500+ terraces

A curated dataset of Amsterdam café terraces, bars, and restaurant terraces — updated constantly.

⏱

Time slider

Scrub through the day to plan ahead. See which terrace peaks at 16:00 before you even leave home.

🔒

Zero tracking

No account, no analytics, no ad SDKs. Your data stays on your device. Always.

How it works

01

Pick a time

Use the slider to set any hour of the day, or let it default to now.

02

Scores update

Each terrace gets a 0–100 sun score based on solar angle, shadows, and cloud cover.

03

Pick your spot

Filter by neighbourhood, tap a terrace for the full-day timeline, and head out.
